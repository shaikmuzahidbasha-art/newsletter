OpenAI Launches Sora 2: Revolutionary Text-to-Video AI

OpenAI officially launched Sora 2, marking a quantum leap in text-to-video generation technology. The new model boasts enhanced temporal consistency, improved physics understanding, and can generate videos up to 60 seconds with cinema-quality resolution. The Sora video app hit 1 million downloads in under five days despite being invite-only and iOS-exclusive.
